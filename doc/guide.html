RELEASE OF SEMESTER I EXAMINATIONS RESULTS

 

Examination results for semester I have been released on

SARIS system. To access your results you

need to do the following:

 

1.   Log on www.ifm.ac.tz

2.   Click on SARIS Login link

3.   Click on Create Account link

4.   Fill in the Registration Form

·       Make sure you type in  your Full Registration Number  (as shown in an example table)

·        Use firstname.surname as your username (eg. Kibakuli.kikombe) use small letters only.

·       Password should have at least five characters

·       Click submit button

5.   When SARIS Login form appears, enter your username and password and press login button

6.   Click on  academic records link

7.   Click on exam results link to see your results

 

In case of any problem, click on Get Help for assistance from

contact details given
